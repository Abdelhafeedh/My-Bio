My Biography

Ibrahim Abdulhafeez

Ibrahim Abdulhafeez is a native of Ilorin,Kwara State,Nigeria.
He is a Muslim by religion.
He attended Radiance Nur/Pry school and upon graduation went to Iqra College before being transferred to ICOHS.
He graduated and got admitted to the Kwara State Polytechnic where he obtained his National Diploma in Accountancy then went to the University of Ilorin to obtain a B.Sc certificate in Economics.
Upon graduation, he was posted to Sokoto for his NYSC program and finally he is now back in Ilorin.
